    • (Linear) algebra deals with solving (linear) systems of equations and the associated aithmetic operations.
    Origin of the term: the word "algebra" comes from the Arabic al-ǧabr / Al-Jabr, which roughly means "reunion" or "comletion".

    The word originally appeared as part of the title of a famous mathematics book by al-Kwarizmi (9th century, Baghdad).
    The full title is: The Concise Book of Calculation by Restoration and Balancing
    (Arabic: الكتاب المختصر في حساب الجبر والمقابلة, al-Kitāb al-Mukhtaṣar fī Ḥisāb al-Jabr wal-Muqābalah)
